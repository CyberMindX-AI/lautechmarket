

# Waitlist Landing Page for SaaS Launch

## Overview
A bold, modern single-page waitlist site to collect early interest before your product launch. Frontend-only — you can later connect a form service (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.) to handle submissions.

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Large, attention-grabbing headline with gradient text
- Short tagline describing the product's value proposition (placeholder text you can customize)
- Animated email input + "Join the Waitlist" CTA button
- Subtle background animation or gradient effects for visual impact

### 2. Social Proof / Counter
- A live-style counter showing "X people already signed up" (starts with a placeholder number)
- Small avatar stack of early adopters for social proof feel

### 3. Feature Highlights
- 3 feature cards with icons previewing what's coming
- Clean grid layout with hover animations

### 4. Footer
- Simple footer with links (Privacy, Twitter/X, etc.)
- "Built with ❤️" tagline

---

## Key Interactions
- Email input with client-side validation (proper email format)
- Success state with confetti or checkmark animation after "submitting"
- Toast notification confirming sign-up
- Smooth scroll animations as sections come into view

---

## Design Direction
- Dark-to-vibrant gradient backgrounds
- Bold typography with gradient accents
- Glassmorphism cards
- Micro-animations on hover and scroll
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
